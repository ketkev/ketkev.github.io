Grand Strategy Game

Grandstrat is a brand new grand strategy game with a large, living and immersive world. The world is inhabited by many AI-based agents, such as the rulers of nations, generals, religious leaders and leaders of merchant collectives. The player plays as one of these characters, and tries to secure their rightful place under the sun.

The game is somewhat influenced by Paradox games such as Europa Universalis 4 and Victoria 2, and games like Civilization.

Right from the start we knew our vision was too large to fully develop in the short period we were given, so we decided that the main focus of this project would be on the technical systems, rather than graphical prowess or well-designed gameplay.
